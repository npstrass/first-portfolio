I designed an educational app to teach nutrition to young children

Role

UX designer creating a mobile app from conception to delivery, including branding.

Problem

Nutrition is overlooked in early education. With two thirds of adults are overweight or obese. Educating on nutrition could help prevent this.

Goal

Create an educational app focused on providing younger individuals educational material on health and nutrition.

Responsibilities

Planning and organizing research efforts, collecting and analyzing data, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, iterating on designs, building in Figma and Adobe Xd.

Product

Cleo is an educational app geared toward teaching children about nutrition and the health benefits associated with a nutritious diet.

Date

2021

Early designs focused on detailed descriptions providing informative material on nutrition. Usability studies revealed users could not follow too much text as the product was targeted towards early education students.

As a result of user feedback, a login option was added to allow for monitoring progress, and text was reduced while images and icons increased. city.
