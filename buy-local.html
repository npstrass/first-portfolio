I designed a website to feature small businesses in Waukesha, WI

Role

UX designer creating a responsive website for local businesses from conception to delivery, including branding.

Problem

Many people visiting Waukesha struggle to find good local businesses that may not get as much marketing as bigger companies.

Goal

Create a site that features rotating local companies to help provide exposure to local businesses.

Responsibilities

Planning and organizing research efforts, collecting and analyzing data, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, iterating on designs, building in Adobe Xd.

Product

Buy Local: Waukesha is a site designed to help consumers find and navigate local businesses in the city of Waukesha, WI. The site lists food, booze, coffee, and more.

Date

2021

Early designs focused on detailed descriptions and a text heavy design. Usability studies found that users thought the overabundance of text was unappealing and difficult to focus on and navigate.

Along with user feedback, a subscription option was added to inform users of additions to the page and new businesses to the city.
